Exercise 3. Fibonacci Fun.

The Fibonacci numbers are defined by \(F_1=1, F_2=2\text{,}\) and \(F_n=F_{n-1}+F_{n-2}\) for all \(n \geq 3\text{.}\)

  1. Prove that \(F_1 + F_2 + F_3 + \ldots + F_n = F_{n+2}-2\) for all \(n \geq 1\text{.}\)

  2. Prove that \(F_1 + F_3 + F_5 + \ldots + F_{2n-1} = F_{2n} - 1\) for all \(n \geq 1\text{.}\)
