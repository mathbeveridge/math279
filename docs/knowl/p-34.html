Let \(x \in \mathbb{R}\) be a real number.

  1. Write down the contrapositive, converse and inverse of the implication: "if \(x < 3\) then \(x^2 < 9\text{.}\)" Which of these four statements are true? Which ones are false?

  2. Write down the contrapositive, converse and inverse of the implication: "If it is warm and sunny then I go swimming."
