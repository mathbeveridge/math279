Solution 1.

We have

\begin{equation*} f \leq \frac{2}{3} e. \end{equation*}

Plugging this into \(v-e+f= 2\) gives

\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{lcr} v-e + \frac{2}{3} f &\geq& 2 \\ v - \frac{1}{3} f &\geq& 2 \\ v - 2 & \geq& \frac{1}{3}e \\ 3v -6 & \geq& e. \end{array} \end{equation*}
